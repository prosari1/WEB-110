My Hobby - Fishing

The reason I love fishing

March 20, 2020

Growing up, my dad always used to take my brother and I to a lake close by our house, so we could fish together. Usually this was my after school actitivty for many many years. I learned how to be more patient and strategic about techniques to make the fish eat my bait.

For me it is a method to relax and appreciate the nature. Also, my dad taught me that we should never kill a fish unless is for feeding someone. That's why I like to catch and release.

Favorite Fish: Pirarara

My favorite kind of fish is a Brazilian species called Pirarara also known as Red Tail Catfish. Something interesting, this fish feeds heavily on live and dead fishes and other meat. It's a kind of fish that cleans the river.
