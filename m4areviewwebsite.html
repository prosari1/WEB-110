M4A - Review a Website

I am reviewing the Living Kitchen website.

  • What is the company/organization? The company is a restaurant with an organic plant-based cuisine.
  • What is the URL? Living Kitchen
  • What is the purpose of the site? The purpose of this website is to reach out to the public about the type of food the serve.
  • Who is the intended audience? Everyone whos is interested in eating healthier.
  • Do you believe the site reaches its audience? Yes, it is well structered website. It has a simple layout and it clearly discloses the type of food the restaurant serves.
  • Was the site useful to you? Why or why not? Yes, it is useful. The Menu is simple and organized. It shows the prices and the food with the ingredients. Very easy to find informations. Nice pictures throughout the website that makes is more convenient.
  • List the colors and/or graphics that are used on the home page of this website Mostly white background and black letter, mixed with high quality images as background and white letters on top.
